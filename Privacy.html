Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

"if you're reading this, you probably used a command and now you're paranoid. relax."

1. Data Collection

We store User IDs and any text you provide via the /remember command. This is saved in a local file called user_data.json.

2. Data Usage

This data is used to make the bot shout your "memories" back at you and to identify your rank. We do not sell this data because nobody would buy your mid thoughts anyway.

3. Developer Access

The Developer (Owner) has full access to the user_data.json file. This access is used for:

  • Ensuring the bot isn't being used to store illegal content.
  • Debugging and technical maintenance.
  • and because some of them are genuinely hilarious.

4. Message Snipping

When you delete a message, the bot sees it and roasts you. We do not save these deleted messages to a permanent database; they are only used for the immediate response.

5. Data Deletion

If you want your data wiped from the JSON file, just use the command to overwrite it or contact the developer. We'll clear it out unless we're busy playing Bee Swarm Simulator.
